GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023/h1>

The Aruba Ruby Global Summit Global Summit brings together the community of developers, academics, and CTO's who comprise the Ruby on Rails network in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of Ruby on Rails, software, technology culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant community together..
